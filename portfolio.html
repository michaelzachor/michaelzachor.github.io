UX: Spotify Community Feature
Fall 2020 | Final Project: User Experience
The final project for my User Experience course was to identify a problem in an existing website or application and design a solution that solves the problem by improving it or adding new features. I used Figma to design a community feature for the Spotify app because the current process of sharing music on Spotify is tedious and requires use of a third party app, like iMessage. The page follows the visual design of the Spotify app and allows users to share music without leaving the app, and keep track of previously shared music.
